My favorite steak marinade from allrecipes.com

Ingredients:
 - 1/3 cup soy sauce
 - 1/2 cup olive oil
 - 1/3 cup fresh lemon juice
 - 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
 - 1 1/2 tablespoons garlic powder
 - 3 tablespoons dried basil
 - 1 1/2 tablespoons dried parsley flakes
 - 1 teaspoon ground white pepper
 - 1/4 teaspoon hot pepper sauce (optional)
 - 1 teaspoon dried minced garlic (optional)

 Directions:
 1. Place all ingredients in a blender.

 2. Blend on high speed for 30 seconds until thoroughly mixed.

 3. Place steak in large zip-lock bag.

 4. Pour marinade over the steak inside of the zip-lock bag and seal.

 5. Place in refrigerator for approximately 2 hours.
